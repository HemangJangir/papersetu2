Quick Start Guide

Welcome to PaperSetu! PaperSetu is your unified platform for academic conference management, submissions, peer review, and collaboration. Whether you are a conference chair, PC member, author, or reviewer, PaperSetu streamlines the entire workflow for you.

From registration to paper submission, review, and final decisions, PaperSetu provides a seamless experience for all users. Join or create conferences, manage roles, submit and review papers, and stay updated with conference news—all in one place.

  1. 1. Register & Login

    Create your account using your email address. After registration, verify your email with the OTP sent to you. You can log in using either your username or email and your password.

    Registration is quick and secure—your academic journey starts here!

  2. 2. Explore Conferences

    Browse available conferences on the Conferences page. Use the search bar to find conferences by theme, venue, city, country, title, or acronym.

    Discover events that match your research interests and expertise.

  3. 3. Join or Create a Conference

    You can join an existing conference or create your own as a chair. As a chair, you can invite PC members and manage the conference workflow, including tracks and reviewer assignments.

    Take part as an attendee or lead your own event with powerful management tools.

  4. 4. Submit Papers

    Authors can submit papers to conferences they have joined. Follow the submission guidelines and upload your paper in PDF format. You can also track the status of your submissions.

    Easily manage your submissions and stay informed about review progress.

  5. 5. Review & Volunteer

    PC members and subreviewers can review submitted papers. You may be invited to review papers or volunteer as a reviewer for conferences. All reviews are managed within the platform for transparency and efficiency.

    Help maintain academic quality and contribute to the research community.

  6. 6. Use the Dashboard

    Track your submissions, reviews, invitations, and conference roles from your dashboard. Stay updated with conference news, deadlines, and manage your academic activities efficiently.

    Your dashboard is your personal hub for all conference activities and updates.

  7. 7. Need Help?

    If you have any questions, suggestions, or need support, please check the FAQs or contact us at papersetu@gmail.com. We are here to help you!

    Our team is dedicated to supporting you at every step—reach out anytime!
